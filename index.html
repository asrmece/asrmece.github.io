About me


My name is Ece Asirim, a data analyst and neuroscience researcher, currently pursuing my Master of Science in Informatics at the University of Zurich with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. My academic journey includes a Master's in Neuroscience from University of Fribourg and a Bachelor's in Bioengineering from Sabancı University. My professional experience spans roles as a Data Analyst Intern at UPK Basel and a Research Assistant in Neuroscience at EPFL and UNIFR, where independently managed multiple research projects and honed my skills in Python, R, MATLAB, SQL, and data visualization along with hands-on scientific experimentation. In my roles as a Neuroscience Research Assistant, I managed intricate datasets and complex analytical tasks and contributed to the field of neuroscience with a publication in Nature Scientific Reports and have developed a strong proficiency in data analysis in the field. As a Data Analyst Intern, I analyzed neuroimaging data and contributed to the optimisation of such data analysis algorithms. Currently, I am working on towards a Master’s thesis on deep learning applications and large language models.
